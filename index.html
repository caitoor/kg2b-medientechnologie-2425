Axolotls are a type of salamander that are native to Mexico. They are known for their unique ability to regenerate lost body parts, including limbs, tails, and even parts of their brain. This remarkable ability has made them a popular subject of scientific research, as scientists hope to unlock the secrets of regeneration in order to develop new treatments for injuries and diseases in humans.
